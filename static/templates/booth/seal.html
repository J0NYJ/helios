Your sealed ballot

You can choose to audit your vote, which will show you how your options were encrypted. If you do so, you will then have to re-vote, since, in a secret ballot, you cannot obtain proof of how you voted.

Your vote has now been encrypted, and the fingerprint of your encrypted vote is:

{$T.encrypted_vote_hash} [Your Receipt]

If you choose to cast the ballot, all plaintext information will be deleted from your browser's memory. Then, you will be prompted for your email and password.
